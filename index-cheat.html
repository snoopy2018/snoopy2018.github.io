Introducing the Artificial Intelligent Card Player

Practice your rational reasoning and take it to the next level. AI Card Player is a computer vision app that assists you to learn and master SET, a card game of visual perception (app runs on an iPhone). It analyses images from camera or file, identifies card features (color, shape, shading, and number), visualizes game rules, and gives hints for finding a set. The app represents an artificial intelligent player or assistant who knows in a group of cards all triplets that form a set.
